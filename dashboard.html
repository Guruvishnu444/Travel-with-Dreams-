Travel Tip of the Day

When visiting the Amalfi Coast, standard buses can get crowded. Consider taking the ferry between Salerno, Amalfi, and Positano. It offers faster transport and breathtaking coastal views from the water!

Also make sure to buy local train tickets online in advance to skip queue queues at Naples Central Station.
